Contact - Corrupt Office Suite Text/
Data Extracting Service - BETA

Upload modern corrupt office suite files for text or data salvaging.  Despite file corruption, it may be possible to avoid retyping or re-entering your data. 

The file uploader currently shows no progress on the screen despite successfully working in the background. Please be patient with large files, such as PowerPoint ones as they may take several minutes to upload depending on connections.

By clicking on the provided recovery links you should be able to view in your browser, a semi-formatted version of your extracted data or text. You can also immediately download this file by right clicking on the link and choosing "Save Target As" (IE) or "Save Link As" (Firefox). Not all the links will work except for DOCX and variant files, see below.

If you are extracting a spreadsheet and you have more than one worksheet, the 2nd and following sheets' data will be in the same first file with the 2nd and subsequent data following the first worksheet's data, with no separator.  For best results copy the data and use "Paste Special." You can view annotated screenshots of the steps involved here.

Click here to recover another document.

The CGI upload script is powered by command line applications from three sources: SILVERCODERS DocToText, Ccy's Corrupt Office 2007 Extractor Command-line and Sandeep Kumar's docx2txt.

Note: DocToText (but not docx2txt or Corrupt Office 2007 Extractor Command-line) works additionally with the Open Office and DOC, XLS and RTF extensions but it's abilities with data/text extraction from corrupt files as opposed to normal ones with these file types may be more limited.
